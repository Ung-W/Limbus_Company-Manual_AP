# Manual AP for Limbus Company

Made by EchoingEito with the help of Evoker

## Goal

You must beat your final Floor

> Can define if you want your final floor to be Floor 5, Floor 10 or Floor 15
 
## Randomization

### Character Related
- All Sinners
- All Sins (status effects)
- Ego slots
- Hyper specific Maost Key (to make mao faust a little harder to reach)

### Gamemode related
- Grace starter buffs
- Ego gift space
- Shop abilities

### AP Gating
- Progressive Floors
  > Party Slots means all the "Sinner Slot +1" items + 1 (this one is implied since you can't go with 0 members in your party)
  - Floor 3+ requires 3 Sinners
  - Floor 5+ requires 3 Sinners and 3 Party Slots
  - Floor 8+ requires 7 Sinners and 3 Party Slots
  - Floor 10+ requires 7 Sinners and 7 Party Slots
  - Floor 13+ requires 12 Sinners and 7 Party Slots
  - Floor 15+ requires 12 Sinners and 12 Party Slots

## Starting Inventory

- One random Sinner
- One Random Sin

## Locations

- Floor 1 has 15 stages and gives 2 checks per Stage
- Floor 2 also has 15 stages but gives 1 check per Stage, onward every floor gives 1 checks per Stage
- Floor 3 and 4 has 10 stages
- Floor 5 to 15 has 5 stages
- One check for beating a floor boss
